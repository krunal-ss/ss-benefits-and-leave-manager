# PRD -- Employee Self-Service Enhancements (v1.4)

Features: 1. Quick Search 2. Recent Activities Widget 3. Leave Policy
Viewer

Quick Search: Search leave requests, expense claims, employees and
policies.

Recent Activities Widget: Display latest employee activities with
filters.

Leave Policy Viewer: View leave policies, eligibility, carry-forward
rules, FAQs and download policy PDF.

Benefits: Faster navigation, reduced HR queries, improved self-service.
